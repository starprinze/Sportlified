Sportified helps schools and tournament organizers manage campus sports competitions digitally in one place.

Instead of updating scores manually across WhatsApp groups and scattered platforms, Sportified allows organizers to:

• create tournaments
• generate fixtures
• update live scores
• manage standings
• run prediction competitions
• engage supporters in real time

Students and supporters can follow matches live, predict outcomes, compete on leaderboards, and stay connected to their department or faculty tournaments from anywhere.

The platform is mobile-first, easy to share, and designed specifically for campus sports communities.

Sportified transforms ordinary tournaments into interactive digital experiences people actually follow.
